Watch February 22, 2026 dissolve and reassemble as particles react to your mouse, visualizing time as a physical substance that erodes and reforms.
"I wanted to create something that treats a specific date as a tangible, physical object — the digits of 2026-02-22 are rendered as thousands of particles that erode like sandstone when you move your mouse near them, then slowly drift back into formation.
